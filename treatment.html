CROSSBITE

A single tooth or a group of teeth can be misaligned with the teeth on each side. Correcting their alignment is critical to avoid bone loss and worn-out teeth.

GAPS

A smile with gaps between one or more teeth might be unpleasant. Closing these gaps can help you speak more clearly and resolve aesthetic issues.

OPEN BITE

While biting, an open bite creates gaps between the upper and lower teeth. Bloom Aligners can help correct this gap.

OVER BITE

An overbite, also known as buck teeth, is a tooth misalignment. When your upper front teeth protrude (stick out) beyond your lower front teeth, you have this condition.

UNDER BITE

An underbite is a dental condition in which your lower teeth protrude further than your upper teeth. It is usually caused by a jaw misalignment.
