Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

An intra-oral scan is performed after any orthodontic abnormalities have been identified in order to establish a Bloom aligner treatment plan for you. Following the creation of the plan, a 3D printed and thermoformed copy of your teeth is created. Polishing and quality assurance are performed on your custom-made aligners at various stages. The aligners are then sent to your orthodontist, who will give you a beginning kit with the aligners.

The time is determined by the orthodontist based on your dental condition. However, depending on the needs, aligner treatments might take anywhere from 6 to 24 months.

A network of Certified Orthodontists at Bloom Orthodontics recommends aligners based on the patient's dental needs. Only a licenced orthodontist is qualified to repair tooth and jaw alignment.

All-Bloom Aligners are made in India. We provide 100% local products.

Because Bloom Aligners are more effective than standard braces, they are more expensive. The actual cost of aligners, however, is determined by a number of factors, including the severity of your disease.

Although aligners do not affect your speech, you may have a lisp in the first few days as your tongue adjusts to them. The lisp disappears within a few days or weeks.

As your mouth adjusts to the aligners, you may experience some discomfort or soreness. It's because the aligners are designed to put pressure on the teeth during treatment. However, as the treatment progresses, you will have fewer and fewer of these episodes.

It is recommended that you wear your aligners all day, except while brushing your teeth, eating meals, or drinking anything other than water.
