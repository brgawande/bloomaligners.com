About the bloom

A simple painless and affordable approach to straighten your teeth has been developed. They can give you a fresh smile, straighten your teeth, fill in gaps in your teeth, and much more. It is used for teeth straightening treatment. An alternative for traditional braces. Typical treatment requires 10-20 sets of aligners and duration of 6-12 months. We create multiple sets for your upper and lower teeth. The aligner adjusts your teeth into the desired position when you wear it.

When deciding on a treatment plan, we should evaluate crowding, open bite, and gaps between teeth. As a result, even if the problem is limited to the upper jaw, the lower jaw must be corrected as well. Both your upper and lower teeth are needed to support your bite.
